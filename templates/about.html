About Crop Yield Prediction Project

Welcome to the Crop Yield Prediction Project! This project aims to predict crop yields in India based on various factors such as crop type, season, state, area, production, annual rainfall, fertilizer usage, and pesticide usage.

Problem Statement:

Farmers face uncertainties in crop yield due to various environmental and agricultural factors. This project addresses the need for a predictive model that can assist farmers in planning and decision-making regarding crop cultivation.
